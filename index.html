Medium:

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Dimension

Description:

Pitorro is a distilled spirit from Puerto Rico referred to as “moonshine rum”. This centuries old spirit dates back to when Puerto Rico’s sugar cane industry was booming, and as time has gone by, it has become a part of Puerto Rican tradition. El Boricuazo is a pitorro distillery that hopes to capture Puerto Rican pride with their branding to share with the world. The scope of the projects was to create custom beverage label designs for the launch of their new products. El Boricuazo launched an original flavor, tamarind, and coquito flavor for the holiday season. Each label was created in a similar style, but also unique to their flavor, and as vibrant as the Puerto Rican culture they want to embody with their product.
