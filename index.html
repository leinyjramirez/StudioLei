GRAPHIC DESIGN

Medium:

Adobe Illustrator

Description:

When it comes to diet and exercise, there always seems to be a new sure-fire method of weight loss out there. Be it Keto, Vegan, Paleo, low-carb, Atkins, or whatever Oprah is doing; there’s something out there for everyone to try. For some, it works! But each body is uniquely made and achieving different fitness goals may not always be achievable with a trendy diet. Knowing the amount of nutrients the body needs to achieve those fitness goals is the best way to notice a change and finally be rewarded for all that hard work. This infographic illustrates how to calculate BMI and the amount of Macronutrients that a person would need on a daily basis to achieve weight loss, maintain current weight, or build muscle.

Medium:

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Description:

The Boston Terrier Rescue of East Tennessee is an established 501 (c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing. rehabilitating and rehoming Boston Terriers. They rely solely on the kindness of their donors and the income of fundraisers, partnerships and grants to sustain their establishment. The organization requested a new social media marketing package to promote their upcoming fundraiser. My branding package included media for "Bark Fest" including a new, vectorized logo, profile picture, cover photo, and banner to be used to further promote the event. I chose to animate the banner to give the page a bit of movement and highlight their adorable mascot Zeke!

BTR was very pleased with the social media packege, and requested a second product be created for their organization. BTR was in need of a full color, tri-fold brochure to represent the organization and their mission to save Boston Terriers. The Brochure was printed in bulk and handed out during fundraising events and distributed to local animal service providers to display in their lobby. The brochure catered to all four divisions of the organization and remained consistent with their new branding. A mockup of the brochure can be seen below.

Medium:

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Dimension

Description:

Pitorro is a distilled spirit from Puerto Rico reffered to as “moonshine rum”. This centuries old spirit dates back to when Puerto Rico’s sugar cane industry was booming, and as time has gone by, it has become a part of Puerto Rican tradition. El Boricuazo is a pitorro distillery that hopes to capture Puerto Rican pride with their branding to share with the world. El Boricuazo was looking for a beverage label design for the launch of their new products. Labels were to cover the full circumference of a custom glass bottle and represent the vibrant culture of Puerto Rico. El Boricuazo launched an original flavor, tamarind, and coquito for the holiday season. Each label was created in a similar style, but also unique to their flavor.
